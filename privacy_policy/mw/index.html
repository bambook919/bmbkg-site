If you create an in-game account independent from Steam, we may collect:

We may also collect:
Game version, language, and crash/error logs.
Gameplay telemetry and anonymous analytics to improve performance and balance.
We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as real names, payment details, or physical addresses).

We use your information to:

We do not sell or rent your personal information.
We may share limited data only in the following cases:
With Steam (Valve Corporation) — to support authentication, multiplayer, and store features.
With service providers — such as hosting, analytics, or anti-cheat providers, who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
With authorities — if required by law or to protect our legal rights.
All third-party partners are required to process data securely and only for authorized purposes.
4. Data Storage and Retention
Your data is stored securely on servers located in the Russian Federation.
We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy.
You may request deletion of your in-game account and associated data (see Section 7).

5. Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption, access restrictions, and secure transmission protocols.
However, no system is completely secure. You acknowledge that you use online services at your own risk.

6. Children’s Privacy
MONSTER WAR is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn that data was collected from a child, we will promptly delete it.

Depending on your country or region, you may have the right to:
