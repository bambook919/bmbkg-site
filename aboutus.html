About us

BMBK Games is an independent indie developer from the Russian Federation. Our games genres are: RPG, Stealth, Horror, Shooter, Action.

At the moment we are developing our debut project - Small RPG. It is a game in the genres of Action, RPG.

We love our players and value them, that's why in our games you won't see the worst traditions of modern game-development: defects, bugs and poor optimization.

We listen to all opinions, and it doesn't matter if it's only one person's opinion or the opinion of a huge number of people.

We are BMBK Games. We make games for you.
