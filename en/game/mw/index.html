MONSTER WAR: What Is It?

Unique Genres Combo

You can not call MONSTER WAR surely the clicker or the role-playing game. This is a game that got iconic elements from both of genres and have some ideas, shapes and play mechanics never met in other clicker games.

Social Activities

There is online mode in this game. You can bargain with other players, have a discuss in in-game chat, use promocodes and get tons of other rewards and bonuses. All your progress is always saved on the game servers — you can get access to it on any available Android or Windows device.

Giveaways by BMBK Games

Server Bless, Weekly Promocode, grand compensations for errors and miscomfortable situations around the game. Active players can always get more.

Great Equipment System

You can buy or craft equipment items, wearing them can speed up your progression by increasing damage, loot drop chances and lowering stamina consumption etc.

Huge Possibilities of Alchemy System

You can craft your own potions using enemies trophies and/or precious metals. These potions will bring you great buffs, but also it may slow your progression. You're the only one who can decide the ingredients combination.

Time-Depend Events

In some days of week you can get Server Bless: it can give you some discounts for upgrading, double your incoming experience and rewards also.

Shadow Market is the    shop available two days a week (Thursday and Sunday). In this place you can get some equipment of quality and exchange the special currency (Shadow Energy) to Sapphires or Magic Stones.

Infinite Replayability

MONSTER WAR gives you the good possibility for endlessly progression: you can always find positions to grow, information to examine and motivation for competitions.
